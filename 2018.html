Sails


Our brief was to create an abstract 3D form that expresses dynamic movement through transition; transition from density to sparsity. We were only able to use a wooden cube, rhino plastic, wire, thread and glue to construct this abstract piece. I chose sails as my precedent and reflected this buy wrapping the rhino plastic around an aluminum rod which stemed from my cube like a mast from a yacht. I also chose to add a thread detailing to relfect the stitching of the sails and create sparsity.



Mimicking Joints


A reflection of the hip. The hip is the joint between the femur and acetabulum which is a ball and socket joint. This multiaxial joint consits of six major movements; flexion, extension, adduction, abduction, medial rotation and laterial rotation. I created hinge and rotation movements within a rounded form to mimick the hip joint.




Phenakistoscope

For this project using photoshop and coding we were creating phenakistoscopes. I chose to create a submarine with a moving shark and people popping in and out of the windows. The final outcome was a physical version and a digital gif.
